About AutoHive

AutoHive is a smart parking solution designed to make parking more efficient and secure. Our project allows users to reserve parking spots for anywhere from 15 minutes to 7 days using advanced QR code technology.

Why Choose AutoHive?

  • Smart, automated parking reservations
  • High-security system with QR code-based access
  • Parking reservations from anywhere, at any time
  • Real-time availability updates to ensure convenience

Innovative Technology

We utilize cutting-edge technology to bring you a seamless parking experience. With our automated system and QR code-based entry, parking has never been more secure and efficient.

Accessibility

Reserve your spot from anywhere, at any time, as long as you have an internet connection. No more wasted time driving around searching for a parking space.

Safety First

AutoHive prioritizes safety. Our QR code system guarantees secure access, and our platform ensures that your reservation is always protected from fraud.

About the Project

This project was created in the Armath Engineering Lab of Yerevan's 146 school in Armenia by the student of this lab, Milena Grigoryan. The Armath Engineering Laboratories are dedicated to fostering innovation and technical skills among students, providing them with the tools and knowledge to create impactful projects like AutoHive.

About Armath Engineering Laboratories

Armath Engineering Laboratories are educational hubs that aim to inspire and educate young minds in the fields of engineering and technology. These labs provide students with hands-on experience and mentorship, enabling them to develop practical skills and work on real-world projects.

About the Creator

Milena Grigoryan, a dedicated student at the Armath Engineering Lab, has demonstrated exceptional skills and creativity in developing the AutoHive project. Her passion for technology and innovation has driven her to create a solution that addresses real-world challenges in parking management.
